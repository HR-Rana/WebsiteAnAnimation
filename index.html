    Why is Strategic Futuring Important Right Now?

    The Genysys Group's founder, Raymond Rood, created Strategic Futuring nearly 40 years ago based on his work in human development and systems theory, the influence of his advisor, sociologist Elise Boulding, and his own experience working with his students.

    He felt it was important for all of his students to graduate with a vision and a plan for the future and after using the process with his students for several years he eventually began using it with private clients and certifying people - like me! - to facilitate Strategic Futuring with individuals and groups.

    One of the things we always say to our clients is change is inevitable but thriving is a choice. The world is experiencing so much change at such a rapid pace now and I've never seen so many people interested in making such big, significant changes in their lives and in their communities.

And if I have a tool I can use to help make people's lives better and help them see that they have control over their future - a tool that will allow them to live life by design rather than by default - I want to make sure as many people as possible have access to it.

    FAQs

    I’ve never heard of Strategic Futuring. Is this something new?

    No, in fact, Strategic Futuring has a rich history! Ray Rood, the founder of The Genysys Group and creator of Strategic Futuring, started using this with his graduate students in the 1980s. It’s grounded in social and human development theory. It’s a time-tested process for developing your vision for your future and a plan to achieve it. Strategic Futuring is different from other planning processes because it addresses your past, present, and future. It’s often exciting to plan for your future, but without taking the time to anchor those plans in where you’ve been and where you are, those ideas are often just dreams. We know that when you anchor your plan in your current reality, building on a solid foundation (which includes your values!), you exponentially increase your odds of achieving your vision. We also know that plans need support. That’s why the development of supportive partnerships (I use that word intentionally - you aren’t asking for support without giving support) is built into the Strategic Futuring process.
    Thousands of people have experienced the power of this process over decades.

    How do I know this course will work for me?

    This course is based on over 30 years of human psychology and development principles. It has worked for thousands of people each with their unique situations, life experiences, and beliefs. While I can’t guarantee that this course will 100% work for every single person who takes it, I’m confident that if you put in the work and follow the process you’ll get clarity on what it is you want for your life and what to do to get there.

And, importantly, you’ll have people to support you, the strategic partners you develop, and the entire Strategic Futuring Community.
